about meed

Meed is a friendly, hands on payroll company committed to offering personalized payroll solutions to ensure that your payroll needs are met.

Meed was started in late 2016 by Natalie MacDonald, a certified payroll consultant who saw a gap in the personalizsed payroll services offered in Northern Ontario.
